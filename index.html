Research Overview

My team's current research on wireless and sensor technologies focuses on four main areas:

Area 1: Wireless Communication

Research on next-generation wireless technologies and protocols for improved connectivity and performance.

Area 2: Health Monitoring

Developing non-invasive sensing technologies for continuous health monitoring and diagnostics.

Area 3: Robotics & Automation

Creating sensor systems that enable robots to better perceive and interact with their environment.

Area 4: Ocean IoT

Building underwater communication and sensing systems for marine research and conservation.
